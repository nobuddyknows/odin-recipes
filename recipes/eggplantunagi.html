Eggplant Unagi

Doesn't theat Eggplant Unagi look delicious!?!? Ever since I went vegan, I have been missing classic Japanese dishes that I grew up loving. One of the dishes was unagi. I thought I'd never be able to have it again. You can imagine my surprise and delight when I found out people have been replacing the eel with eggplant to make a vegan variant, and let me tell you, it is DELICIOUS! Let's break down this recipe y'all!

Ingredients

  • 3 Japanese Eggplants
  • 1tbsp Mirin
  • 3tbsp Shoyu
  • 1tsp Sake
  • 1tbsp Sugar

Instructions

  1. Microwave the eggplants for 4 minutes. Let them cool. Then, slice them open in a way where you can flatten them.
  2. Put some oil on a pan and heat on medium-high. PLace the eggplant skin-side down and cook for a couple minutes. After a couple minutes, flip the eggplants over.
  3. Add the sake and mirin, and let it cook for 45 seconds. Then add the sugar and soy sauce(save these for lasts so tehy won't get burned). Continue cooking for another minute.
  4. Serve on a bed of rice and top it off with sesame seeds and green onions if you want.
