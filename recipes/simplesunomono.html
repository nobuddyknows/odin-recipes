Easy Sunomono

Here is a super simple and super quick Japanese cucumber sunomono recipe! Anyone can make it, and it only take 15 minutes! UNBELIEVABLE!!

Ingredients

  • 2 Japanese Cucumbers
  • 3tbsp of Ponzu
  • 1 clove of pressed garlic
  • Sesame Seeds

Instructions

  1. Thinly slice cucumbers
  2. Let the sliced cucumbers sit in a bowl of salt water for 5 minutes. Then drain and dry the cucmbers well.
  3. In a ziplock bag, add the ponzu and pressed garlic. Then add the cucumbers and mix them around. Let them marinate for 10 minutes.
  4. They are ready to be served! Top with sesame seeds.
