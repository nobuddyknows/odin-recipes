My Favorite Smoothie Recipe

I'm gonna show y'all my favorite smoothie recipe because obviously you guys care about that right??? It's super easy and flavorful. The secret master tip is to use frozen fruits! ;*

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen banana
  • Handful of frozen berries
  • Handful of frozen strawberries
  • A lil bit of oats
  • 1 tablespoon of smooooooth peanut butter
  • 2/3 cup of milk of your choosing
  • Oh yeah and some spinach so we can make it "healthy"

Instructions

  1. Put everything in a blender.
  2. Blend.
  3. Serve.
